Documentation

Learn how Co-Intent works and get the most out of our platform.

Getting Started
How It Works
Co-Intent is a platform for collaborative digital creation. Group buyers pool resources to commission digital products, then share in future revenue from sales of those products through our buy-to-earn model.
Connecting Your Wallet
To participate in orders and receive revenue shares, you'll need to connect a compatible cryptocurrency wallet. We support MetaMask, WalletConnect, and other Ethereum-compatible wallets. Revenue is distributed in USDC stablecoin.
Participating in Orders
Finding Orders
Browse available group orders in the Marketplace. Each order specifies the product being commissioned, total budget, minimum number of buyers needed, and share price (cost for 1% ownership stake).
Joining an Order
Once you find an order you want to join, click "Join Group" and specify your desired contribution amount. You'll receive an NFT certificate representing your ownership share, which entitles you to a percentage of future revenue.
Revenue Sharing
How Earnings Work
When a product you've invested in generates sales, revenue is automatically distributed proportionally to all shareholders. 70% of each sale goes to shareholders, 20% to the platform, and 10% to the executor who created the product.
Smart Contracts
All orders are managed by immutable smart contracts on the Ethereum blockchain. These guarantee transparent and automatic execution of revenue distribution according to the agreed terms.

Support Center

Need help? We're here for you.

Frequently Asked Questions
How do I contact support?
You can reach our support team by emailing support@co-intent.com or through the live chat feature in the bottom right corner of the screen (available 9AM-5PM EST).
How long do withdrawals take?
Crypto withdrawals are processed within 24 hours. Bank transfers typically take 3-5 business days. Some verification may be required for larger withdrawals for security purposes.
Is my investment safe?
While we take every precaution to vet orders, like any investment platform there is risk involved. We recommend diversifying across multiple orders and carefully reviewing all project details before committing funds.
